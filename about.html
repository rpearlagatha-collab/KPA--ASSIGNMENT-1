About me

KWIKIRIZA PEARL AGATHA

My name is Kwikiriza Pearl Agatha. I am a certified midwife serving with Ankole Local Maternity and Neonatal Systems and the Program Lead at Mother Mentor Uganda (MMU). My calling is integrating Midwifery Models of Care into Uganda's national maternal health policy, ensuring every mother receives skilled, dignified, and compassionate care. Beyond my professional life, I am a student, a mother, a daughter and a voice for those who are rarely heard.
